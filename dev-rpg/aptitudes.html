Aptitudes and leveling system

This is a no class RPG. Skills rely on one or more aptitudes, and you can level up every aptitude. The power of a skill you use will be function of your level in aptitudes this skill relies on. You gain xp in aptitudes by using skills linked to them, but when you level up an aptitude, all others aptitudes become harder to level up. The amount of XP needed to level up an aptitude is global. You first need 10 xp to level up an aptitude. Each time you level up an aptitude, the amount of xp needed for the next level increases by 2.
The aptitudes bellow which have no description are not yet implemented. I currently plan to implement 31 aptitudes, but this number may evolve.

Attributes (4 aptitudes)

Constitution

Determines your amount of heal point (HP). You regenerate 100% of your life within 50 sec. You earn 1 XP in this aptitude per HP regenerated.

Strength

Determines your amount of stamina point (SP). You regenerate 100% of your stamina within 50 sec. You earn 1 XP in this aptitude per SP regenerated.

Intelligence

Determines your amount of mana point (MP). You regenerate 100% of your mana within 50 sec. You earn 1 XP in this aptitude per MP regenerated.

Dexterity

Determines your chances to avoid an attack, spell or projectile. You earn 1 XP in this aptitude per attack dodged.
